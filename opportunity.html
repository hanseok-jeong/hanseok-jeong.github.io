Opportunity

Openings

We are looking for highly motivated researchers for the following openings.
Please contact the PI, Hanseok Jeong (hanjeong@seoultech.ac.kr).

PhD Student (Graduate Research Assistant)

  • Benefits: Competitive stipend sufficient to cover living expenses in Seoul, with full tuition support.
  • One international conference participation per year will be supported for meaningful research outcomes.

Master’s Student (Graduate Research Assistant)

  • Benefits: Competitive stipend sufficient to cover living expenses in Seoul, with full tuition support.
  • One opportunity to attend an international conference during the master’s program will be provided.

Undergraduate Research Intern

  • Benefits: Flexible participation in ongoing lab research activities with a high degree of autonomy.
  • A stipend is provided based on engagement level.
  • Opportunities to attend domestic conferences and, for impactful research outcomes, international conferences.
