Lab Leader

HANSEOK JEONG
Lab Head · Associate Professor

Hanseok Jeong leads the Environmental Intelligence Laboratory at Seoul National University of Science and Technology. His research focuses on environmental intelligence, socio-hydrological systems, physically-based modeling, and data-driven decision support for sustainable water and environmental management.
