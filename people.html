Lab Leader

HANSEOK JEONG

Associate Professor, Department of Environmental Engineering, Seoul National University of Science and Technology (SeoulTech)

Hanseok Jeong is the head of the Environmental Intelligence Laboratory (EIL) at SeoulTech, where his research focuses on socio-hydrological modeling, environmental data analysis, and the development of AI-driven frameworks for environmental management. His current research explores the fusion of physically-based hydrologic models and machine learning to enhance decision-making in complex water systems.
